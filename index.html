Funding for Continuous Operations and Development

The ZenCash Treasury fund is generated by 8.5% of each block mining reward and is used for operations, development, and getting out the message of Zen to the World.

Treasury funds are held in trust and allocated by the Zen Blockchain Foundation, a non-profit entity entrusted with growing and maintaining the Zen ecosystem.

Zen Secure Node operations is encouraged and funded by a 3.5% redirection of block mining reward.

Communicate Any Type or Size of Information

Zen private messaging integrates with IPFS (https://ipfs.io/) for distributed publishing and information access.

IPFS is the Distributed Web. A peer-to-peer hypermedia protocol to make the web faster, safer, and more open.

Any type of information in multiple formats can be published to IPFS, then linked to and messaged by Zen private and secure communications.

Integrating with IPFS Helps Build the Internet’s Next Web

IPFS remains true to the original vision of the open and flat web, and delivers the technology which makes that vision a reality.

IPFS powers the creation of diversely resilient networks which enable persistent availability with or without Internet backbone connectivity.

IPFS provides historic versioning (like git) and makes it simple to set up resilient networks for mirroring of data.
